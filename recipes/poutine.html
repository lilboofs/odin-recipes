Poutine Recipe

This poutine is made by cooking fries until crispy, slathering a roasted turkey or chicken gravy, and topping with cheese curds.

Ingredients needed:

  • Fries
  • Gravy
  • Cheese

  1. Fry the fries until golden brown and crispy.
  2. Make the gravy, hot and slathery
  3. Top the whole thing with fresh cheese curds from Quebec
